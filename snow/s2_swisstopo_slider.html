General info - Swiss Topo & recent Sentinel S2 imagery

This page could be useful when planning a trail run or hike early in the season, to check if there might still be some snow remaining on your planned route.

I only see clouds

Try selecting another date under the dropdown menu on the top right. Currently no cloud filtering is applied, in order to retain as many pixels as possible.

How up to date is the imagery?

Data are refreshed at 8.15am daily. Sentinel S2 has a revisit time of ~5 days.

I don't see any satellite imagery

If you're unlucky, Sentinel hasn't captured data for your place of interest. Try selecting another date under the dropdown menu on the top right. If some smaller tiles are missing try zooming in and out to reload data.

Disclaimer / small print

Please note that this is not a real snow detection tool, it just displays the latest RGB imagery. Snow covered by debris, underneath tree canopy or close to rock faces might not be visibile. Clouds, buildings or other bright pixels can appear as snow. Be careful and take none of these results for granted.
